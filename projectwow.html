PROJECT WHISKERS OF WAR

WIP • Development Begins April 2026

The Story

As a housecat in a seemingly peaceful town, your world is ripped apart when the town itself becomes a warzone. You must rely on your feline agility and instincts to navigate the chaos, embarking on a desperate journey of survival through the horrors of war.

Core Gameplay

Fluid Movement (WIP)

Responsive cat movement that will allow for fluid traversal. I am aiming for an experience that is interactive and engaging.

Exploration & Survival

Climb, sneak, run! Experience environmental storytelling told through a linear progression of levels that will take you through the war-torn town and its surrounding areas. Each area will have its own unique challenges, secrets, and dangers to overcome.

Parkour (WIP)

Utilize parkour to navigate an urban landscape reshaped by conflict.
